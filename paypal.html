Setup Paypal Payment

1. Register for paypal account at Paypal.com .

2. Login to your paypal account.

3. Go to developers page https://developer.paypal.com/developer/applications.

4. Scroll down to REST API apps and click Create App

5. Type Roompillow on App Name field and click Create App

6. On the next page, click on the Live and take note of the Client ID and Secret

7. Go to Roompillow dashboard » Settings. Click on the Booking tab.

8. Key in the Paypal Client ID and Paypal Secret and click Save .
