Frequently Asked Question
Do i need myallocator account?

Only if you want to sync booking automatically with online booking website. Roompillow use Myallocator handle the data connection with many online booking channels. You can register for myallocator account at www.myallocator.com.

What is the requirement to run Roompillow?

You need the latest firefox or google chrome browser and a good internet connection with minimum 1024x800px screen resolution.

How to cancel my account?

You subscription will automatically cancelled once you stop paying subscription fees.

What payment method are accepted?

Only paypal supported at the moments.

Do i need to pay additional for Myallocator?

Yes. Myallocator is a third party service provider.
