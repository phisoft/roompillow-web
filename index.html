About Roompillow

Roompillow is a cloud-based property management platform designed for small to medium sized hostels, hotels and B&Bs. It has a built in channel manager that connects to Myallocator channel manager which integrate with with 80's of other OTAs.

The solution also includes a drag-and-drop reservations calendar for front desk staff, and an online booking engine for a property's website & Facebook booking supports.
